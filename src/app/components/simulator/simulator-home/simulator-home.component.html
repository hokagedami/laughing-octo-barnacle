Guide

  • The toolbar contains the gates and inputs that can be used to build a circuit.
  • Click on a gate to add it to the center canvas. Ensure the center of the canvas does not have any gates.
  • Right-click on a gate to remove it from the canvas.
  • The gates are draggable and can be connected to other gates when on the canvas.
  • The gates can be connected to each other by clicking on the connector gate placed before the reset button, which turns yellow to show the connector is active. Then click on the input/output circle of a gate to begin connecting and click on the input/output circle of another gate to complete the connection. The connection line will be drawn between the two gates, and the input/output circles will turn red to show the connection is successful.
  • The canvas can be reset by clicking the reset button.
This simulator is not available on small screens. Please use a larger screen or a computer.
