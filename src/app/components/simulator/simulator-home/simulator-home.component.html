Guide

  • The toolbar contains the gates and inputs that can be used to build a circuit.
  • Click on a gate to add it to the center canvas. Ensure the center of the canvas does not have any gates.
  • The gates are draggable and can be connected to other gates when on the canvas.
  • The inputs can be connected to gates by dragging a line from the input/output circle of one gate to the input/output of another gate.
